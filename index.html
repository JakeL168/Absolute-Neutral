Absolute/Neutral

DESCRIPTION:

An experimental math-based turn-based game where you observe enemy's stats,

pick your own ATTACK / DEFENSE value, and use it on the enemy.


Tips: The defend part is a little tricky,

as you can only ever neutralise enemy's attack,

and whatever's left will be minus from your HP.

CONTROLS:

LEFT / RIGHT: Choose ATT/DEF value

Z: Confirm choice

X: Restart battle ( When DEFEATED / BEATEN )
